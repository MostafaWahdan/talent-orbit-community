# Deliverable Template

Write `deliverable.md` in the run directory using exactly this structure. A founder should be able to read it cold. Style: confident but provenance-explicit, no em-dashes, no hyphens as punctuation, no filler. Every confidence claim states its N. In `work_sample` mode, replace "questions for the hiring team" framing with "assumptions I made and what I would verify first".

---

# Talent Market Map: <Role> at <Company>
<Date> · Method: incumbent calibration with ring expansion · Evidence legend: [O] observed from a cited source, [I] inferred with stated basis

## 1. Executive summary
5 to 8 lines. What the calibration revealed, how many companies and anchors were verified, the one or two findings that should change how this search is run, and the biggest open question.

## 2. What good looks like (calibration profile)
Based on N = <n> in-role profiles ([O] count vs [I] count). The pattern: scope, formative environments, stack depth, trajectory shape. State confidence plainly; if the low_confidence flag is set, lead with it.

### JD delta (when a JD exists)
| Confirmed bar (JD and incumbents agree) | JD only (likely the intended delta for this hire) | Incumbents only (unwritten requirements) |
|---|---|---|

If no JD was available, one line saying the map is built on revealed preference only.

## 3. Hypotheses and their status
For each: statement, status (supported / weakened / open), evidence count, and what would change our mind. Dead hypotheses stay listed with one line on what killed them; the negative result is information the client paid for.

## 5. Coverage gaps and bias flags
Mandatory, never skip. Concentration flag and the diversification quota applied, thin-footprint companies that could not be characterized honestly, low N caveats, geographies or segments the method systematically under-covers here.

## 6. Verification questions for the hiring team
Numbered, sharp, ordered by leverage. Each question traces to a delta, a weakened hypothesis, or a flag. Aim for 5 to 8.

## 7. Recommended next actions
Concrete: which tier to source first and why, 3 to 5 ready search strings, suggested outreach order, what a second pass would add if commissioned.

## Appendix: method and evidence standards
One short paragraph: incumbents as revealed preference, two-engine expansion, verification bar (1 named anchor, 2+ calibration attributes, observed source), stop conditions. Then: rings expanded, API calls used out of budget, run date.
